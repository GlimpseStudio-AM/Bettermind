Got questions? We've got answers!

Check out our FAQs to learn more about
BetterMind Therapy and how we can support you.

What is BetterMind Therapy?
BetterMind Therapy is an online platform that connects you with licensed therapists who provide professional mental health support via video, audio, or text sessions.
How do I choose a therapist?
You can browse our extensive directory of therapists, filtering by specialty, availability, and ratings. Each therapist has a profile detailing their experience and approach to therapy.
What types of therapy are offered?
We offer various therapy options, including individual therapy, couples therapy, family therapy, and specialized support for issues like anxiety, depression, trauma, and more.
Is my information safe and confidential?
Yes! Your privacy is our priority. All sessions are conducted in a secure, HIPAA-compliant environment, ensuring your information remains confidential.
Can I change my therapist?
Absolutely! If you feel that your current therapist isn’t the right fit, you can easily browse and switch to another therapist at any time.
Do you accept insurance?

While we do not directly accept insurance, many of our therapists can provide you with a receipt for reimbursement from your insurance provider. Check with your provider for more details.
